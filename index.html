h-CoV-19 Variants of Concern Browser2

This application was developed as a final project for course 410.712 Advanced Practical Computer Concepts for Bioinformatics.

The h-CoV-19 virus emerged in December 2019. Since then it has rapidly spread around the globe acquiring mutations and leading to the development of viral variants. Some of these variants are classified as "Variants of Concern" due to their increased transmissibility, antibody evasion, and increased disease severity. This application functions to display the distinguishing mutations found in the current variants of concern.

Use the forms below to explore the distinguishing mutations found in h-CoV-19 Variants of Concern.

  • Data can be displayed in one of two ways: two variants can be selected for comparison, or all the genes may be selected to view the mutations found on each.
  • Due to the ever-evolving nature of this pandemic, this tool displays the latest mutations and variants as of the timestamp at the bottom.
  • The variants are denoted using their PANGO nomenclature, but countries of origin will be referenced as well.
