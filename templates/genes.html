Display of Genes and their Mutations

  • Click on the genes below to display the variant mutations.
  • Note that there are many mutations found on any given h-CoV-19 virus, these are specifically the distinguishing mutations that identify variants of concern.
  • The 'Mutation Type' is denoted with 'aa' for amino acid replacement or 'del' for nucleotide deletion.
  • Polypeptide length is denoted as 'various' when there are alternate protein lengths depending on proteolytic cleavage.
